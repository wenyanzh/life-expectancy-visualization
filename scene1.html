Life Expectancy vs. GDP per capita

Back to Intro Next Slide

From the chart, we can see the breakdown of life expectancy vs. GDP per capita of each country. Some highlights of the chart are:
  • Japan has the highest life expectancy, but its national GDP per capita is not the highest. USA has higher GDP per capita than Japan, but life expectancy is lower.
  • Lesotho has the lowest life expectancy, but not the lowest GDP per capita. However, countries with some of the lowest life expectancy (< 55 years) all have low GDP per capita.
  • Countries in Africa all have life expectancy lower than world average (72 years), despite two countries have higher than world average GDP per capita.
  • Asia has the most diverse range of life expectancy and GDP per capita.

    • Note: GDP per capita is in log scale to better visualize the skewed data. Circle size indicates size of country population. World average is the pink dot in the middle.

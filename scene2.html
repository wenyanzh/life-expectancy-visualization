Life Expectancy vs. Health Expenditure per capita

Previous Slide Next Slide

From the chart, we can see the breakdown of life expectancy vs. health expenditure per capita of each country. Some highlights of the chart are:
  • USA has the highest health expenditure per capita, and it's significantly higher Switzerland, the country with second highest health expenditure per capita. However, life expectancy in USA is not the highest, and it's 4 years lower than Switzerland.
  • Central Africa Republic has second lowest health expenditure per capita, and Democratic Republic of Congo has the lowest health expenditure per capita. Interestingly, Central Africa Republic has one of the lowest life expectancy (52 years), but Democratic Republic of Congo's life expectancy is significantly higher by 7 years.
  • Again, Asia has the most diverse range of life expectancy and health expenditure per capita.

    • Note: Health expenditure per capita is in log scale to better visualize the skewed data. World average is the purple dot in the middle.
